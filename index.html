About Me

Hey, thanks for looking into me. My names Nathan Ramirez. I moved around a lot, and have probably stayed in around 12 houses now. I'm used to change, and am plenty able to adapt. I grew up loving nature, and I hold true to that this day. To me, a beautiful sunset is one of the greatest things that someone can see. I had long hair all my life until I cut it through High School. I still have longer hair than normal, but much shorter than I constantly kept it when I was younger. I work out six times a week, and plan to continue until my bones break trying to lift another weight. Later in life, I want to travel to other countries and see what they all have to offer. I have four siblings, with me being the youngest, but of course, the greatest. Saved the best for last and all that jazz. When I was younger I was much more introverted, but now it doesn't really matter. I don't mind any music, as long as it is good. Good music is good music, no matter if it's rock, rap, country, or jazz. My favorite artistt is The Weeknd, but especially his older music. I grew up in California and plan to stay inside for as long as I'm able. I enjoy reading, writing, and hanging out with friends. I'm a quick learner and strive to have 'perfection' everywhere. I enjoy cars, science and ecology. I like to help people feel better about their day, and hope that people always leave with a good impression of me after any interaction. I wish to make it in this field, and will try again and again to make it happen. Look forward to seeing you!
